Open Close Robotics & Automation ‚Äî Picking, Packing, Sorting, AGVs

Quirk Facilities (Context)

  • Quincy, MA: 200,000 sq ft, multi-level.
  • Manchester, NH: 200,000 sq ft, multi-level.
  • Objective: higher throughput, lower labor per order, fewer errors.

What MOPAR Is Doing (Pattern)

  • High-density grid (AutoStore-style) with robots retrieving bins.
  • Robots feed human pack stations for QC, packing, shipping.
  • Benefits: speed, accuracy, compact footprint; scale robots up as volume grows.

Your Options (Layered)

  • Packing Automation: fit-to-size carton makers + pack stations ‚Üí labor ‚Üì, corrugate/void fill ‚Üì, DIM weight ‚Üì.
  • Sorting Automation: modular robotic/cross-belt sorters ‚Üí high items/hr, near-zero mis-sorts, peak smoothing.
  • AGVs/AMRs: tote/pallet moves between floors/zones, 24/7, safer than forklifts, scalable fleets.

How AI/ML (Machine Learning) Fits

  • Vision & ID: camera/AI for item verification, box selection, damage checks.
  • Dynamic slotting: ML assigns fast movers to optimal locations; walk time ‚Üì.
  • Forecasting: ML for demand ‚Üí min/max & reorder proposals auto-generated.
  • Flow orchestration: WES/WMS uses ML to balance waves, robots, and stations.

Business Wins (Summary)

  • Labor savings from fewer touches and automated transport.
  • Throughput & cycle-time gains (more lines/hr, later ship cutoffs).
  • Error & return reduction via machine-verified sort/pack.
  • Space efficiency; delay building expansions.
