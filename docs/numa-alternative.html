NUMA Alternative

A dealership-ready AI voice agent that handles Sales, Service, and Parts — integrates with VIN Solutions & DMS, escalates safely, and supports English & Spanish.

Get a Demo Open Project Library

What it does

Tier-1 call handling with tool-driven actions: create CRM tickets, hold service slots, quote trades, and check order status. Short, professional voice style; graceful escalation when needed.

Where calls go

  • Sales → Lead + Activity (+ optional Appointment)
  • Service → Vehicle link (VIN/YMMT) + Service Appointment
  • Parts → Quote lines + optional Pick-up/Ship setup

Voice Capabilities

  • Expressive TTS with natural prosody, multilingual EN/ES, and multi-speaker mode for training.
  • Live streaming to telephony with retries and fallback vendor path when errors occur.
  • Governance: PII minimization, correlation IDs, retention, and audit tags.

Powered by Google AI Studio’s native speech generation (free/unlimited at time of writing), with a configurable fallback.

VIN Solutions — create_ticket

Lead/Activity creation with x_correlation_id. Optional appointment. Minimal PII in logs.

schedule_service

Hold a service slot, capture concern text & transport flags, and link vehicle by VIN/YMMT.

quote_trade

Capture trade snapshot (Y/M/M/T, VIN, mileage, condition) and return an appraisal range + follow-up URL.

check_order_status / Parts Quote

Build line items by part # or description; set fulfillment method/time and pickup appointment if needed.

CRM/DMS Field Mapping (High-Level)

  • Common: store call_id, timestamps, duration, DNIS, recording_url, consent flags, and intent tag on the Activity.
  • Sales: Lead upsert with Vehicle Interest, trade snapshot, financing intent; optional Appointment.
  • Service: Link VIN/YMMT + mileage + concern text; tag warning lights & transport; create Service Appointment.
  • Parts: Quote lines + fulfillment method/time; optional Pickup Appointment.
  • Identity resolution: exact match on phone/email/VIN; avoid dupes with Needs Merge when ambiguous.

Escalation & Safety

  • Escalate if confidence < 0.6, caller is upset/toxic, legal/financial specifics arise, or a human is requested.
  • On vendor errors: retry with backoff; if still failing, tag tts_fallback and/or send ADF/XML to CRM inbox and mark Needs Merge.
  • Always capture and store consent flags; attach the call recording link to the Activity.

Metrics We Track

  • FCR (First Contact Resolution)
  • Deflection Rate (AI vs Human)
  • CSAT
  • AHT (Average Handle Time)
  • TTS/ASR latency (p95), talk-over & barge-in rates

Rollout Snapshot

  • Weeks 1–2: TTS adapters + streaming bridge; feature-flag EN voices
  • Week 3: Spanish enablement & IVR language switch; QA scripts
  • Weeks 4–6: Multi-speaker training audio; guardrails & fallbacks; A/B voice styles; publish CSAT/FCR deltas
