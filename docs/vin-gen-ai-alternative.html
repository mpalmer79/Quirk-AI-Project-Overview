Inventory & Incentives Integration

AI reads live website inventory and OEM programs, then injects tokens into responses while keeping work inside VIN.

Data & Endpoints

  • Inventory: GET /inventory.json (stock_number, vin, year, trim, status, price, photo, updated_at)
  • Incentives: GET /incentives?make=&model=&zip= (cash_rebate, apr, term, expiry)
  • Tokens: [[stock_count]], [[model_trim]], [[cash_rebate]], [[apr_rate]], [[two_appt_options]], [[primary_photo]]

Event Flow

  1. New lead arrives in VIN
  2. AI pulls inventory + incentives
  3. Composes email/SMS (<5 min) with two slots
  4. Logs Activity + creates Task/Appt in VIN
  5. Handles replies; sends confirmation/reminder
Open for more (fallbacks & SLAs)
If inventory feed unreachable → serve cache ≤15 min & flag “stale”. If incentives timeout → omit incentives line, keep appointment CTA. Price/availability mismatch → add “subject to prior sale” + closest alternative.

FAQ

Will AI replace our BDC agents?

No. AI drafts, nudges, and keeps tasks moving; people build trust and close. The system removes repetitive work so agents can focus on live conversations and appointment-setting.

What AI handles

  • Composes personalized emails/SMS with inventory & incentives in under 5 minutes.
  • Creates/updates VIN Solutions activities, tasks, and appointments so nothing slips.
  • Sends 24/48-hour re-nudges and day-7 manager escalation, consistently.
  • Provides real-time talk tracks for common objections (payment, availability, “just shopping”).

What agents do (human-in-the-loop)

  • Approve/modify outbound messages when needed and make the actual calls.
  • Handle pricing nuance, trade-value conversations, and relationship building.
  • Confirm appointment details, coordinate vehicles, and close for the show.

Quality guardrails

  • Manager-visible audit trail in VIN Solutions for every touch.
  • Safe defaults when data is missing (e.g., omit incentives line; keep appointment CTA).
  • Clear disclaimers (“subject to prior sale”) and alternatives when exact vehicle isn’t available.
Bottom line: AI standardizes great habits (speed, personalization, consistent appointment pushes). Your team keeps control and spends more time talking to customers.
Does everything stay inside VIN Solutions?

Yes—VIN Solutions remains the system of record. The AI layer writes back every interaction so reporting and accountability live in one place.

What’s written to VIN

  • Activities: full message body (HTML note), who/when, and any tokens used.
  • Tasks: auto-created follow-ups with due dates to maintain compliance.
  • Appointments: proposed times and confirmed start/end once the customer accepts.
  • Email thread sync: replies are associated to the lead record for a complete history.

Why this matters

  • No “shadow CRM”—lead status, touch count, and outcomes are accurate in VIN.
  • Managers can coach from a single timeline (speed to first touch, nudge cadence, outcome).
  • Reporting (conversion, show rate, SLA adherence) stays intact for the BDC and Sales.

Controls & security

  • Respects VIN permissions/roles; agents only see what they should.
  • Every AI action is attributable (who triggered, when, and what changed).
How do incentives and inventory stay accurate?

Live data, with sensible fallbacks. The system reads your website inventory feed and OEM program endpoints before composing a message. If anything is unavailable, it defaults to safe language that still drives an appointment.

Primary sources

  • Inventory feed: GET /inventory.json (VIN, stock#, year/make/model/trim, status, price/MSRP, colors, primary photo, updated_at).
  • Incentives API: GET /incentives?make=&model=&zip= (cash rebate, APR, lease cash, term, expiry, disclaimer).

Response logic

  • Pulls fresh data at compose time (target end-to-end < 5 minutes from lead creation).
  • Maps values into templates (e.g., [[stock_count]], [[apr_rate]], [[cash_rebate]], [[two_appt_options]]).
  • If feeds time out or are missing values, the message omits specifics and keeps the appointment CTA.

Accuracy & fallbacks

  • If the inventory feed is unreachable, serve cache ≤15 minutes old and flag “stale” in the note.
  • On price/availability mismatch, add “subject to prior sale” and propose the closest in-stock alternative.
  • Agents can override any line item before sending (human-in-the-loop).
Source of truth: VIN on the lead record + your website’s live feed. Messages are designed to be accurate by default and cautious when data is uncertain—without losing momentum toward an appointment.
