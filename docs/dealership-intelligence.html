System Overview

Our Smart Workplace Verification system integrates facial recognition technology with your existing biometric time clock system to ensure employees are present in appropriate areas during work hours. Designed specifically for automotive dealership operations with compliance-first architecture.

12 IP Camera Network

Comprehensive coverage across entrances, sales areas, service departments, and showroom with intelligent area-based monitoring.

Biometric Integration

Seamless sync with your existing fingerprint time clock system to cross-reference attendance data.

Department-Based Access

Role-specific area permissions ensure Sales, Service, and Management staff are monitored appropriately.

Automotive Compliance

30-day data retention, admin audit trails, and industry-specific security protocols built-in.

Intelligent Alert System

Our if/then logic engine provides contextual alerts based on employee status, location, and time:

Verified Presence

Employee clocked in + detected in appropriate area = Normal verification logged

Unauthorized Area

Department mismatch alerts (e.g., Sales staff in Service area)

Time Clock Discrepancy

Employee present but not clocked in = Potential time theft alert

After-Hours Detection

Security alerts for unauthorized after-hours presence
