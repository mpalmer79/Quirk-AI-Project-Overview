The Quirk AI Workplace Verification system integrates facial recognition with your biometric time clock to ensure employees are present in appropriate areas during work hours. Built for dealership operations with a compliance-first architecture. Camera feeds identify individuals against an employee photo database to verify presence, correlate with timecards, and monitor movements by area and workstation.
