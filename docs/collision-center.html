Why add AI-powered chat to Collision pages?

  • Capture after-hours leads: 24/7 responses convert late-night inquiries to scheduled estimates.
  • Faster first reply: Sub-minute answers lift estimate → RO conversion; less phone tag.
  • Triage & routing: Directs photo estimates, rental/insurance, and ADAS calibrations to the right queue.
  • Lower workload: Automates FAQs/status so advisors focus on repairs.
  • Compliance & visibility: Conversations can push into CRM/DMS for tracking.

  • Hardware: AutoStore robotic warehousing; UVeye AI inspection portals; AR overlays.

Automation & Integration
