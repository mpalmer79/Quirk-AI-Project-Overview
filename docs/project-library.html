Automated Follow-up & KPIs

Appointment-first cadence aligned to VIN tasks. Faster replies, higher conversion, tighter compliance.

ExpandCollapse

Why it matters

  • Speed: Sub-5-minute replies win the first touch and set the tone.
  • Consistency: Prebuilt touches prevent “lead drift” and uneven follow-up.
  • Compliance: Tasks/appointments stay inside VIN for full visibility.

14-Day Follow-up Cadence

  • Day 1: Immediate personalized Email + SMS (inventory, incentive, two time options).
  • Day 2: Phone call attempt (AI objection prompts).
  • Day 3: Follow-up Email: trim/options + quick value props.
  • Day 5: SMS nudge: “Still interested?” + appointment push.
  • Day 7: Manager escalation touch (credibility + urgency).
  • Day 10: Alternatives (similar vehicles or payment route).
  • Day 14: Final chance / closeout incentive + two time options.

Weekly Ops Rhythm

  • Monday: Review desklog funnel (leads → contacted → appts → shows → deals).
  • Mid-week: Listen to two calls/rep; coach on appointment-first prompts.
  • Friday: Scorecard update; highlight wins, stuck leads, and next week’s tests.

Manager Tips

  • Make “two time options” mandatory in every first touch.
  • Escalate to a named manager on Day 7 (don’t skip).
  • Use desklog gaps (e.g., high “No Attempt”) to trigger call blocks the same day.
