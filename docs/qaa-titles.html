Plan & Architecture

Practical, low-risk automation first.

Digital Title Vault

  • Secure scan intake → OCR → schema validation (VIN, owner, brand).
  • Status flags: clean, lien, salvage; mismatch alerts.
  • Role-based access; audit events.

RPA Swimlanes

  • Auto bill of sale & title transfer at time of sale.
  • Overnight jobs: invoices, gate passes, receipts.
  • DMV site updates where allowed; otherwise queue for clerk review.
