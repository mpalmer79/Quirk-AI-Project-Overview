Creatio Copilot centralizes generative AI use cases across the Creatio platform with no-code setup and embedded AI in Sales, Marketing, and Service.

Creatio Copilot: A centralized hub within the Creatio platform for configuring, deploying, and managing generative AI use cases. It works as a virtual assistant across all Creatio products, helping users with tasks like:

  • Summarizing sales opportunities and dashboards.
  • Generating personalized content for emails and marketing campaigns.
  • Providing real-time assistance and suggestions for resolving service cases.

AI agents and skills: Creatio supports various AI agents and skills that automate tasks using OpenAI and other models. Users can create custom AI skills with no-code tools and natural language commands.

Embedded AI for CRM: The integration powers AI functionality directly within Creatio's CRM suite, including:

  • Sales: Summarizing leads and opportunities, generating communication templates, and scoring leads.
  • Marketing: Assisting with audience segmentation, campaign flow design, and content generation.
  • Service: Providing real-time assistance for case resolution, summarizing sentiment, and helping with knowledge management.

AI-assisted app development: Creatio's no-code platform allows users to leverage generative AI to create applications from a simple description. The AI pre-populates templates for views, data models, and business rules, accelerating development.

Marketplace connectors: While AI is natively integrated, Creatio also offers specific connectors for OpenAI, including a ChatGPT Connector on its Marketplace that allows for AI-driven content generation.

Privacy and security: Creatio emphasizes robust security, using technologies like Azure OpenAI services to ensure that customer data remains private and is not used to train AI models.

Fullpath integrates with OpenAI to power AI-driven marketing and customer engagement for automotive dealerships via its CDP.

Expand Collapse

Fullpath integrates with OpenAI to offer AI-powered marketing and customer engagement tools for the automotive industry. As part of its Customer Data Platform (CDP), Fullpath uses OpenAI's Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate personalized customer experiences based on a dealership's data.

Key features of the integration

Enhanced customer data platform (CDP): Fullpath's CDP is the foundation of its AI capabilities. It consolidates data from a dealership's CRM, DMS, inventory, website, and ad platforms. This allows the AI to generate hyper-personalized content for customers.

  • AI-driven email marketing: Fullpath uses OpenAI-powered AI to create and run email marketing campaigns.
  • Custom campaigns: Dealers can use the CDP's first-party data to define specific audience segments and send them targeted, personalized emails with content generated by the AI.
  • Automated content generation: The AI assists dealerships by creating personalized content that can be seamlessly integrated into their existing marketing automation workflows.
  • ChatGPT integration: Uses dealership data to create a holistic and personalized conversational experience for customers.
  • Customized chatbot: A custom GPT trained on inventory, OEM offers, and local promotions for a tailored chatbot experience.
  • Safety measures: Guardrails such as system-level prompts and disclosures ensure the chatbot provides appropriate and transparent responses.

How Fullpath uses AI with OpenAI

  • Data consolidation: Aggregates CRM, DMS, inventory, site, and ad data into one platform.
  • Data activation: Uses consolidated data to train and activate AI for personalized content.
  • Campaign creation: Dealers define segments and goals in Fullpath's tools.
  • AI generation: LLMs generate targeted, engaging content—e.g., emails—for those campaigns.
  • Execution and tracking: Automates delivery and measures performance to show impact.

Salesforce CRM is a cloud-based platform that unifies sales, service, marketing, and commerce on a single integrated platform with AI-driven insights and automation.

Expand Collapse

Salesforce CRM is a cloud-based customer relationship management platform that helps businesses manage all aspects of customer interaction across sales, service, marketing, commerce, and more, providing a unified view of the customer on a single integrated platform. It uses AI and analytics to provide actionable insights, automates processes, and offers customizable tools to help companies increase productivity, personalize customer journeys, and ultimately drive growth.

What it is:

  • A cloud-based platform for managing customer relationships and streamlining business processes.
  • A suite of connected apps, known as “clouds” (Sales Cloud, Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, Commerce Cloud), that unify customer data into a single view.
  • A tool to attract prospects, nurture leads, close deals, support customers, and market to them effectively.

Key features and benefits:

  • Centralized Data: Brings together all customer data into one platform, creating a single source of truth.
  • Sales Automation: Helps sales teams manage leads, track opportunities, and close deals faster.
  • Marketing Tools: Provides features for personalized and automated marketing campaigns across various channels.
  • Customer Service: Tools for managing support tickets, building knowledge bases, and providing live chat.
  • AI-Driven Insights: Uses AI to deliver actionable insights and support data-driven decisions.
  • Customization: Customize fields, objects, and workflows to meet specific needs.
  • Increased Productivity: Collaborate better, automate manual tasks, and focus on higher-value work.

Tekion integrates with OpenAI and has a partner relationship with PBS Systems.

Integration with OpenAI

  • Azure OpenAI Service: Tekion uses OpenAI's technology within its Automotive Retail Cloud (ARC) via Microsoft’s Azure OpenAI Service.
  • Customer Communication: “Smart Communication” analyzes and summarizes calls, chats, and emails between customers and sales agents.
  • Sales Recommendations: Provides agents with customer-specific recommendations and prompts to deepen engagement.
  • Customer Experience: Generative AI streamlines the purchasing process to make the experience more fluid and efficient.

Integration with PBS Systems

  • DMS Connectivity: Integration enables shared customers to connect data between Tekion and PBS Systems.
  • API Partner: In March 2025, Hammer announced new API integrations with both Tekion and PBS Systems.
  • Data Sharing: Partnership improves data flow and lead handling for dealerships using both platforms.
  • Open Platform: Tekion’s Automotive Partner Cloud (APC) allows third-party vendors and partners to build direct integrations.
